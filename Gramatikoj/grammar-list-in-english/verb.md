Verbs
=====

Conjunctions
------------

* Infinitive: Stam + -i
* Tempo & Mood: 
1. Present: + -as
1. Past: + -is
1. Future: + -os
1. Subjunctive / Conditional: + -us
* Participle: 
1. Present: + -ant
1. Past / Passive: + -at NOTE: passive sentences do not use aux verb; use forms -atas, -atis ... instead. One can have passive infinites -ati.
